Employee Management System

Angular · Spring Boot · PrimeNG

A full-stack operations portal for a fictional organization: people, structure, and day-to-day tasks, with the menu and screens changing by role so each persona sees what they are allowed to do.

  • Sign-in and roles. Users authenticate at a dedicated login, then land in an app shell with a sidebar. Everyone gets Dashboard and tasks; only managers and administrators see Departments, Designations, and the full Employees directory.
  • Dashboards. Administrators and managers see organization-wide metrics (counts and KPI-style cards). Individual contributors see My dashboard: how many tasks are open, how many are due soon, and a short list of recently completed work.
  • Departments and job titles. Authorized roles maintain a clean org chart in software form: create, review, edit, or remove departments and designations using table-based lists and modal dialogs so updates stay structured.
  • Employee records. Build and maintain the team directory: assign department and job title, capture hire date, link a person to an existing user account or onboard someone new, and pick their access level (employee, manager, or administrator) when adding them.
  • Tasks. The sidebar label switches between My tasks and Tasks depending on permissions. Team members track assigned work with priorities and status; leaders get a broader view suitable for assigning and updating work items using sortable, filterable tables and detail dialogs.

Flavor Haven

Angular · Spring Boot

A food-discovery and planning product: browse and search a large recipe catalog, save what you love, and lay out meals across the week—wrapped in a simple signed-in experience with Home, Search, Meal Planner, and Liked in the sidebar.

  • Home discovery. Start from visual tiles for popular dishes, meal types (breakfast, main course, snacks, drinks), and world cuisines. Each choice loads a results grid; open any card for a full recipe view with rich information to support cooking decisions.
  • Search and refinement. The Search area supports free-text lookup plus practical filters—vegetarian, vegan, keto, gluten-free, maximum cook time, and a calorie ceiling—and sorting by popularity, cooking time, health score, or cost. Quick suggestions and recent searches make repeat exploration faster.
  • Liked recipes. Signed-in users maintain a personal collection: saved recipes load in one place, open into the same detail experience, and can be removed when you no longer want them on the list.
  • Meal planner. Plan a real week: for each day assign breakfast, lunch, and dinner by searching the catalog and slotting recipes. Move between weeks, persist your plan, and reuse saved week templates so recurring planning takes fewer clicks.
